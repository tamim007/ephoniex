What are the basic requirements for usernames?

New usernames have two basic requirements:
They must be at least five characters long and start with a letter
Only letters and numbers should be used; no special characters, spaces nor symbols
All usernames at E-phoenix are unique. If you try to pick one that is already in use, that username will be rejected
